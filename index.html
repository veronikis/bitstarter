Save on energy costs

The use of SmartWalls® can save costs for heating/cooling and lighting in numerous ways. It can easily replace motorized light screens such as blinds and curtins. When set to opaque mode it blocks most UV radiation into the building, thus keeping it cooler and safer. When set to transparent mode it allows in natural light from the ambient environment, thus reducing lighting costs. It can also be combined with adjustable light bulbs (e.g., LEDs with dimmer capability) to reach an optimal point of natural and artificial lighting in a room.

By using SmartWalls® you can create smaller rooms that are to be heated/cooled to the level the user desires, without creating a closed-to-a-cage feeling. Thus, smaller areas that are not currently used can be heated/cooled to lower/higher temperature, resulting in electric energy savings.

As shown on the graph on the left, the use of SmartWalls® on a company's premisses occupying nearly 750m2 resulted in energy savings of approximately 7% within the first year.

Change your point of view

By replacing conventional walls with SmartWalls® you remove sight-blocking objects, thus creating the illusion of being in a bigger room. Therefore, people living/working in the newly created room do not feel restricted or confined. The additional light shed into the area and the extended line of sight make the users of the room feel more comfotable. To further enhance this feeling, at your request we can provide you with a special version of a SmartWall® panel that comes ready to accept a wall-type aquarium.


Maintain Privacy

When used in meeting rooms, director's office or a bedroom/bathroom SmartWalls® can be set to opaque, thus providing to persons in the room privacy, which they can revoke at the press of a button.

Easy installation

With the SmartInterLocking (SIL)® system, one can easily connect wall panels with each other, creating a configuration that best meets the needs of the area. The SIL® system enables you to easily attach a door to a SmartWall®. When the transparent surface does not need to be large, the SIR® system can be used to connect a SmartWall® to other surfaces that can be fabricated by durable, light, opaque and eco-friendly material.

No special electricity skills are required to install them. Just plug 'n play into you wall outlet.


Can I afford it?

The cost of the technology used is nowadays affordable for both houses and companies and the installation of SmartWalls® returns on the investment as it saves money from energy savings. In addition, as the market grows more manufacturers are claiming a piece of it, thus leading to more economical and efficient materials.


Is it safe?

SmartWalls® are not dangerous. They are safe for people (kids, elderly persons and visitors) since they operate on low voltage, thus preventing electrocution. Also, SmartWalls® are durable, build from thick glass (such as the one used in shower cabinets) to sustain any strong hits, should a person or an object hit the walls.

FAQ

What is the technology used?
SmartWalls® consist of electrochromic devices. These have the ability to change light transmission properties in response to voltage and thus allow control over the amount of light and heat passing through. In electrochromic windows, the electrochromic material changes its opacity: it changes between a colored, translucent state (usually blue) and a transparent state. A burst of electricity is required for changing its opacity, but once the change has been effected, no electricity is needed for maintaining the particular shade which has been reached.
